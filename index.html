Bio

About Me

Hello! I am a PhD student in Artificial Intelligence for Climate Change at INRIA-Paris joint with Sorbonne University. I work with Prof. Claire Monteleoni in the Climate & Machine Learning Boulder-Inria Team (CLIMB-IT) .I graduated from the West African Science Service Center on Climate Change and Adapted Land Use (WASCAL) Graduate Study Program with a MSc. in informatics for climate change.

My research interests revolve around the intersection of climate science and machine learning particularly, the applications of deep learning algorithms in the context of green energy transition. My research focused on renewable energy forecasts and the impacts of extreme weather on future demand . My long-term goal is to build innovative and efficient AI-based solutions for a sustainable development.

I like reading, watching games and play sometimes .
